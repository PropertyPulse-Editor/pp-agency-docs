Privacy Policy

Effective date: 26 June 2026

PP Agency is the official employee application for Property Pulse, owned and operated by Exodus Media. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and manage information when authorized employees use PP Agency.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

This application is for authorized employees, field agents, administrators, and approved representatives of Exodus Media. It is not a customer-facing application.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on the assigned role and workflow, PP Agency may process the following information:

  • Employee account details such as employee ID, name, role, login status, and activity statistics.
  • Authentication data required for secure employee login through Supabase Auth.
  • Customer information entered by agents, including name, phone number, address, booking type, subscription plan, payment status, and notes.
  • Document verification images uploaded by authorized employees, such as Aadhaar, utility bill, voter ID, driving license, or other proof documents.
  • Operational data such as customer history, call logs, subscription records, timestamps, and admin review data.

3. Authentication and Account Security

PP Agency uses Supabase Auth for employee authentication. Passwords are managed by Supabase Auth and are not stored in application database tables. Administrators may create employee accounts, reset temporary passwords, and require password changes where necessary.

4. Camera and Gallery Access

The application may request access to the device camera or photo gallery so authorized employees can capture or upload customer verification documents. These permissions are used only when an employee chooses to capture or upload an image for business verification purposes.

5. Document Uploads and Storage

Uploaded document images may be compressed before upload and stored in Supabase Storage. Document metadata may be stored in Supabase PostgreSQL. Access is restricted based on employee role and Row Level Security policies where configured.

6. Location Data

PP Agency may request location permission to assist employees in autofilling address-related fields such as street or area, city, state, and pincode during customer registration. Location access is used only for this operational purpose and only after permission is granted by the user.

7. Biometric Login

The app may offer biometric login, such as fingerprint or face authentication, where supported by the device. Biometric verification is handled by the device operating system. PP Agency does not collect or store fingerprint, face, or biometric templates.

8. Permissions Used

Camera, Photos/Media, Location and Biometric permissions are used only when required for authorized employee work, including document capture, document selection, address assistance, and secure sign-in. These permissions are never used for advertising or marketing.

9. How We Use Information

We use information to register subscriptions, verify customer documents, manage employee activity, maintain customer history, support administrative reporting, secure user access, and operate the Property Pulse subscription process.

10. Data Sharing

Data is used internally by Exodus Media for Property Pulse operations. We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers used to operate the app, including Supabase for authentication, database, and storage infrastructure.

11. Data Security

We use role-based access, Supabase Auth, database security policies, secure storage practices, and administrative controls to protect information. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we work to reduce unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure.

12. Data Retention

Business records may be retained for operational, legal, accounting, audit, and dispute-resolution purposes. Records may be deleted or restricted when no longer required, subject to company policy and legal obligations.

13. User Rights and Requests

Employees may contact Exodus Media to request access, correction, review, or deletion of eligible account information. Customer-related requests should be handled through authorized company procedures and applicable law.

14. Children

PP Agency is not intended for children and is not directed to users under the age of 18.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this website with a revised effective date.
