PP Agency for Property Pulse field operations

PP Agency is the secure internal application used by authorized Property Pulse employees to register customers, verify documents, manage subscriptions, and support field operations.

PP Agency is available exclusively for authorized Android devices used by Property Pulse employees.

Secure Access

Employee login, session controls, password management, and optional biometric sign-in help protect operational access.

Agents can register customer details, select subscription plans, upload verification documents, and record payment status.

Admin Oversight

Administrators can review employee activity, customer records, operational targets, and exported reports.

Purpose

Designed for internal business use

PP Agency supports newspaper subscription operations for Property Pulse, India's real estate weekly newspaper brand. The application is intended for employees of Exodus Media and authorized representatives only.

  • Customer registration and history
  • Document capture from camera or gallery
  • Location-assisted address entry
  • Secure storage through Supabase services
  • Employee and admin workflows
